Last 48-Hour Cram Plan

  1. Morning: ports + OSI + subnetting drills.
  2. Afternoon: troubleshooting cards + PBQ scenarios.
  3. Evening: security + wireless + cloud terms.
  4. Final night: light review only. No panic-studying until 3 AM.

Exam-Day Checklist

  • Bring valid ID and arrive early.
  • Memorize ports, OSI layers, private IP ranges, APIPA, and troubleshooting steps before starting.
  • Flag long questions and move on. Do not let one ugly PBQ steal your time.
  • For troubleshooting questions, start with the simplest likely cause first.
  • When two answers seem right, choose the one that is safer, documented, and least disruptive.
